Shelbie Caro

Softball Coach


I am a Freshman softball player at UC Riverside. It was my dream to play softball ever since I was very young. I am an only child so I have had a lot of pressure growing up, but I always embraced the pressure. The road to get to play at the collegiate level was not an easy journey. It took a lot of hard work and determination to get to where I am but I could not be more proud of myself now. I have been playing softball ever since I was six years old. I started playing in a little league in my city. Honestly, at first I did not like it and wanted to quit immediately. Luckily my mom and dad did not let me quit in the middle of the season. I stuck it out and decided I wanted to continue playing. Fast forward until I was nine years old. I started playing on my first travel softball team. I have played competitive softball and have never missed a tournament. I have played on three teams which were CA Breeze, Lady Magic, and CA SunCats. I have not always had the best experience, but that has made me into the person I am today. I have made some of my best friends from playing softball. Although there have been many times I wanted to quit, I never did and I am so grateful. I have worked with a variety of coaches and players over the years. I play first and third base while being known as a power hitter. I want to help young kids who have the same dream that I had. I know without my coaches I could not be where I am today. I want to be a good influence to allow children to follow their dreams the same way I did.
